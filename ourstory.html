Our Story

The Beginning

Started by educators and entrepreneurs who saw the gap in real-world learning for youth, NextGen Bosses was born to inspire, equip, and launch teen entrepreneurs. What started as a summer camp has grown into a movement.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every teenager has the opportunity, skills, and confidence to pursue their entrepreneurial dreams. A world where young people are not just consumers, but creators, innovators, and leaders who shape the future of business and society.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to building the next wave of innovative leaders by teaching business, money management, and leadership in ways that are fun, relevant, and bold. We believe that entrepreneurship isn't just for adults—it's for every teen with vision, voice, and passion to create change.

Our Values

Creativity Over Conformity

We celebrate unique thinking and encourage teens to break free from traditional molds.

Faith-Driven Purpose

We believe that meaningful businesses are built on strong foundations of purpose and values.

Action Over Perfection

We believe in learning by doing. Perfect is the enemy of done.

Leadership With Integrity

True leadership is about serving others and making ethical decisions.

Community Over Competition

We foster a supportive community where teens help each other succeed.
